BAWAH ISLAND RESORT

Located on a small archipelago, the Bawah Island Resort is a remote paradise off the coast of southern Malaysia.

A RARE ECOSYSTEM EXPERIENCE

Indulge yourself in an environment where nature has inspired both our design and our service. Our staff think and act holistically, and our healthy meals draw ingredients from nearby environments whenever possible. We care about our ecosystem and we welcome our guests to participate in our conservation efforts to protect this amazing environment.

ONLY 160 NAUTICAL MILES FROM SINGAPORE

Only accessible by seaplane, Bawah is true barefoot luxury, a sanctuary to slow down and unwind in. Your package includes full round-trip transfers from Singapore, daily meals (breakfast, lunch and a la carte dinner), soft drinks, wellness centre and surface water sports. No hidden costs.

GETTING HERE

Your journey begins at 7am (Singapore time) with a luxury car from your home, hotel or airport in Singapore, to Tanah Merah Ferry terminal. Here you will be met by a member of the Bawah team and escorted on a short ferry ride to Batam,Indonesia. With snacks and cold drinks provided. Fast tracked through immigration we then introduce you to our travel coordinator, Vio, who will guide you through the rest of your journey. Starting at our private lounge in Batam airport, where you will then take a 70-minute flight aboard our private plane, an amphibious plane, with 2 pilots, that takes off on land and then arrives on water, in the beautiful lagoon right in front of your suite. Your exciting journey will take around 3 1/2 hours.
